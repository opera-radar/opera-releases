### CIRRUS v-1.3.5
* Introduction of new reflectivity data from:
  * 1 new radar new site in Norway (_Rássegálvárri_)
  * 1 new radar old site in Ireland (_Shannon_)
  * 7 new radars old sites in Romania (_Bucuresti_, _Oradea_, _Craiova_, _Timisoara_, _Bobohalma_, _Barnova_ and _Medgidia_)
  * 2 new radars new sites in Poland (_Góra Św. Anny_ and _Gdynia-Szemud_)
  * 2 new radars old sites in Poland (_Ramza_ and _Brzuchania_)
  * 1 new radar old site in Croatia (_Puntijarka_)
  * 1 existing radar with fixed quality of data in Denmark (_Bornholm_) → completed on 12/11/2024 
  * 1 existing radar with the ‘best’ volume data in Belgium PAGW (_Widemont_) → completed on 18/12/2024
* Reintroduction of satellite filtering for:
  * all 11 radars in Finland 
* Correction of the coordinates of a radar location for:
  * 3 existing radars in Slovakia (_Maly Javornik_, _Kojsovska hola_ and _Spani laz_)
* Removal of 1 radar in Poland (_Gdańsk_)
* Metadata in CIRRUS product (HDF5-ODIM file):  
  * introduction of the ODIM manadatory attribute _/dataset1/what/prodname_
  * update of the ODIM attribute _/how/comment_
* Bugfix:
  * strengthening control of the BALTRAD pre-processing to prevent disruption of the production in case of corrupted incoming files
